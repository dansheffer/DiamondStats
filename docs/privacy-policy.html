Diamond Stats

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Diamond Stats ("the App") is a free baseball statistics viewer developed by Daniel Sheffer. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

  • No account or sign-up is required.
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information are collected.
  • No location data is accessed or stored.
  • No cookies or tracking pixels are used.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores a small amount of data locally on your device to improve performance:

  • Favorite team preference — your chosen "My Team" selection.
  • Cached API responses — temporary copies of scores, standings, and player stats to reduce network requests. This cache is automatically cleared and never leaves your device.

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App connects to the following external services to display baseball data:

  • MLB Stats API (statsapi.mlb.com) — for scores, standings, player statistics, and schedules. This is a publicly available API. Your IP address may be visible to MLB's servers as part of standard internet communication.
  • MLB.com RSS feed — for news headlines and article links.

Advertising

The App may display non-personalized banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads do not use personal data for targeting. You may remove ads via an optional in-app purchase.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional tip-jar purchases and an ad-removal purchase. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal information from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
