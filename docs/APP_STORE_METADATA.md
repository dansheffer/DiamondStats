## Description (4000 chars max)

Diamond Stats brings you real-time MLB scores, in-depth player statistics, and advanced sabermetrics — all in a beautifully designed, free app.

**LIVE SCORES & BOX SCORES**
• Today's games updated in real time with scores, innings, and game status
• Tap any game for a full box score with individual batting and pitching lines
• See who got the W, the L, and the save at a glance

**PLAYER CARDS**
• Search any current or historical MLB player
• Bio info: age, height, weight, bats/throws, debut date
• Season and career stats for both hitters and pitchers
• Two-way player support (hitting + pitching side by side)
• Year-by-year career snapshots

**ADVANCED SABERMETRICS**
• Calculated FIP (Fielding Independent Pitching) and wOBA (Weighted On-Base Average)
• Season and career advanced metrics: BABIP, ISO, K%, BB%, K/9, BB/9, HR/9, and more
• Understand the stats that front offices actually use

**STANDINGS**
• Full divisional standings with W-L, PCT, GB, home/away records, run differential
• Wild Card race view with real-time WC rankings and clinch scenarios
• Last 10 games and streak tracking

**MY TEAM**
• Pick your favorite team for a personalized dashboard
• Quick access to your team's upcoming schedule and results

**NEWS**
• Latest MLB headlines pulled directly from official sources
• Tap to read the full story

**SEARCH**
• Find any player — active or historical — by name
• Active/retired badges help you find who you're looking for

**BUILT FOR iPHONE & iPAD**
• Responsive design optimized for both iPhone and iPad
• Liquid Glass design language with frosted glass effects
• Fast, smooth, and beautiful

Diamond Stats is free with no account required. An optional tip jar lets you support independent development. A small, non-intrusive ad banner can be removed with a one-time purchase.

Data provided by the MLB Stats API. Diamond Stats is not affiliated with or endorsed by Major League Baseball.
